What is a CTF?

A Capture the Flag (CTF) is a competitive cybersecurity event where participants solve security-related challenges to find hidden "flags," which are specific pieces of data, within systems or code. These challenges test a variety of skills, such as cryptography, web exploitation, reverse engineering, and network analysis. CTFs are often used in educational settings or competitions to help individuals and teams practice and demonstrate their hacking and problem-solving abilities.If you're having trouble finding a flag, click the hint button for assistance.
